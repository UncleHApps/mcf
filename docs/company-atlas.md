# Mas Freight: Company Atlas

## 1. Company Identity & History
- **Entity Name:** Mas Freight (Strictly use "Mas Freight", never "Maschem Freight" or variants).
- **Established:** 1991. Founded with a single truck by the current director's father.
- **Director:** Masa Kuguyo.
- **Locations / Operations:**
  - **Johannesburg Headquarters:** Unit A, 38 Monteer Road, Isando, Gauteng 1600, South Africa.
  - **Harare Operations:** 24 Conald Road, Graniteside, Harare, Zimbabwe.

## 2. Core Services & Specialisations
- **Primary Service:** Cross-Border **Full Truck Load (FTL)** Freight. The company explicitly does not co-load or consolidate cargo (no brokering).
- **Regions Served:** South Africa, Zimbabwe, Zambia, and Malawi.
- **Fleet Capacity:** Maximum load weight of 34 Tons. Transport supports 20ft and 40ft containers, pallets, crates, drums, flowbins, and bags.
- **Target Industries:** Mining & Industrial (including hazardous chemicals), Agriculture & Farming, Construction & Manufacturing, and general Import/Export.

## 3. Value Proposition & Differentiators
- **Asset-Owned Fleet:** Mas Freight is an asset-owning carrier, not a broker. "Own the Truck, Own the Result." This provides clients a direct line to the drivers and complete accountability.
- **Budget Certainty:** Dedicated FTL quoting ensures absolute budget certainty with no surprise fees or arbitrary "hazchem premiums."
- **Customs Advantage (RIB):** Licensed as a Remover in Bond (RIB). This facilitates seamless cross-border trade without upfront VAT and duty bottlenecks.
- **Predictability & Reliability:** Perfected Beitbridge clearance system. Predictable direct transit times (e.g., from Johannesburg to Zimbabwe in 5-7 days, Zambia in 7-9 days, Malawi in 7-10 days).

## 4. Brand Tone & Messaging Guidelines
- **Tone of Voice:** Professional, direct, authoritative, reliable, and grounded in decades of real operational experience.
- **Spelling Convention:** South African English (e.g., specialisation, centre).
- **Key Phrasing:** "Predictable Timelines. Budget Certainty. Cross Border Freight Delivered."
- **Core Values:** Unwavering Safety, Steadfast Reliability, Client-First Approach, and Integrity.
