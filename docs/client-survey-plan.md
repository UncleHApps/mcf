# Mas Freight: Simple Client Survey (5th Grade Level)

This survey is for our clients. We want to know how we can make your life easier. We don't care about fancy words. We want to know what happens to your business when shipping goes wrong. 

We will use your answers to make our service better and fix our website.

---

## Part 1: Easy Questions to Start
1. **What do you mostly ship with us?** (Example: Mining chemicals, farm tools, or food?)
2. **How many times a month do you send a full truck across the border?**
3. **Where do your trucks go most often?** (Example: Harare, Lusaka, or Lilongwe?)

---

## Part 2: What Matters to You (The Real Stuff)

### 4. The Biggest Headache
**Question:** *"Before you found Mas Freight, what was your biggest problem with shipping across the border?"*

### 5. Why You Switched
**Question:** *"Was there a specific time when another company messed up so badly that you decided to leave them? What happened?"*

### 6. What Matters Most
**Question:** *"What is the most important thing to you: Being fast? Being cheap? Or always knowing where the truck is?"*

### 7. When Things Go Wrong
**Question:** *"If a truck is late or your stuff gets broken, how does that hurt your business? Do you lose money? Does a customer get mad? Tell us what happens."*

### 8. Talking to the Boss
**Question:** *"We own our trucks. You can talk to us directly. Does that make your job easier? How?"*

### 9. Boring Paperwork
**Question:** *"What part of the paperwork or office work is the biggest pain for you? What do you hate doing most when it comes to the forms and documents for shipping?"*

### 10. Better Service
**Question:** *"What is one thing you wish we told you more often while the truck is on the road? (Like a text every morning, or a map link?)"*

### 11. The "Hard Part" Question
**Question:** *"If we closed our business tomorrow, what part of your job would become a big headache again?"*

### 12. Your Honest Review
**Question:** *"In your own words, what is it really like to work with us? Please be honest. Your words help other people know if they can trust us."*
