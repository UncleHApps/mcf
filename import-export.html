The RIB License Advantage for Exporters

As a licensed Remover in Bond (RIB), we provide a significant streamlined advantage for your exports from South Africa. This license allows us to suspend VAT and duties while your cargo is in transit to the border.

Improved Cash Flow: No need to pay VAT upfront and wait for SARS refunds.

Operational Speed: Bonded cargo moves faster through border posts like Beitbridge and Chirundu.
