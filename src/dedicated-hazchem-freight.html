High-Stakes Cargo Demands a Specialist Partner

Transporting hazardous materials across international borders is a zero-tolerance environment. Incorrect documentation, non-compliant vehicles, or untrained drivers can lead to catastrophic delays, fines, and risks to your reputation and the public.

Our direct-asset, FTL-only model is the foundation of our hazchem service. By eliminating third-party risks and ensuring a single, unbroken chain of custody, we provide the highest level of control and compliance for your high-stakes cargo.

Our Specialized Hazchem Capabilities

The Remover in Bond (RIB) Advantage

As a SARS-licensed Remover in Bond, we offer a significant financial and operational advantage for your exports. This license allows us to move your goods "in bond" directly from your collection point to the final border exit, suspending VAT and duties through South Africa.

This eliminates costly delays and complex refund processes, giving you peace of mind and improved cash flow.

Financial Certainty & Improved Cash Flow

By suspending VAT on your export, you don't have to pay it upfront and then reclaim it from SARS, freeing up your capital and avoiding the slow, administrative burden of the refund system.

Operational Speed & Peace of Mind

Bonded cargo is pre-cleared for transit, meaning your dedicated truck moves through the border post without lengthy customs stops. We control the entire process, reducing third-party risk and delays.
