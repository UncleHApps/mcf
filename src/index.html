Our Promise, Forged in 1991

A note from the director:

In 1991, my father started this business with one truck and a simple, powerful promise: be the most reliable name on the road. For years, he perfected that promise with general freight, building a reputation for unwavering dependability.

As we grew, we saw a persistent challenge in the market. We realized that for high-stakes cargo, true reliability wasn't just about a good driver—it was about owning the entire process. The market needed a partner who could provide a direct, unbroken chain of custody from collection to delivery.

That realization created the Maschem Freight of today. I'm proud to carry on my father's original promise by providing a level of certainty the market was missing. We treat every high-value load with the absolute expert care it requires, because for us, that professional standard is the only way to deliver true reliability.

Masa Kuguyo, Director of Maschem Freight, a direct-asset logistics company

Your Questions, Answered

From Johannesburg, typical delivery to main centres:

  • Zimbabwe: 5-7 days.
  • Zambia: 7-9 days.
  • Malawi: 9-11 days.

FTL means your cargo, and only your cargo, is on the truck. No co-loading.

  • Max Load Weight: 34 Tons (inclusive of container weight if used)
  • Suits: 20ft & 40ft Containers, Pallets, Crates, Drums, Bins, Bags.

Maschem Freight provides the haulage service: the truck and a trained, professional driver.

Loading and unloading is typically handled by the client at collection and delivery points.

  • First-time clients: Prepaid before loading.
  • Returning clients: Credit facilities available (subject to approval).

Our safety record speaks for itself. We're fully insured as legally required in all countries of operation.

Clients are welcome to arrange additional Goods in Transit (GIT) insurance at their own cost if desired.

Our trucks depart from Isando (Johannesburg, SA) or Harare (Zimbabwe).

Free collection within a 50km radius of these depots. Standard rates apply beyond that.
